Meet the Owners



A Long History
Serving the Wonderful State of Wyoming

Robert and Lorene Weinrich purchased 412 E. 5th in 1953 and years later opened Weinrich's Frozen Food Lockers, Inc.

The business expanded to wild game meat processing, locker storage, and wholesale/retail meat sales.

The three sons, Tommy, Jimmy, and David ran Weinrich's Frozen Food Lockers and the Wine Rack restaurant.

In 1989, Jimmy and David started Bullwhip Catering. Bullwhip Seasoning was soon to follow.

Over the years, numerous generations of the Weinrich family have worked alongside their parents, brothers, sisters, cousins, grandchildren, and friends.

The Weinrich's have donated to many organizations over the years including the Mission, Meals on Wheels, Boys & Girls Club, and the VFW just to mention a few

Thank you to our customers, business associates, friends, and family across the great state of Wyoming. We have enjoyed many caterings and appreciate your continued support over the last 68 years.


Thank you, The Weinrich Family
